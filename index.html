chalana

Chalana is a type of vessel commonly seen in the Pantanal, a Brazilian biome which is also the world's largest tropical wetland. Surrounded by beautiful landscapes, chalanas are always transporting people and objects across the rivers. And it is with this spirit that the Chalana store decided to navigate, delivering pieces of art full of meaning to different destinations and promoting the richness of Brazil's cultural identity.

cultural value

Every product that Chalana delivers has relevant cultural significance. A common tradition in inland cities, the ex-voto is a symbolic object of a promise or request offered to a deity. Some churches still have the so-called "Miracles Room", where all the ex-votos of the faithful community are deposited. Built in wax, wood, ceramics or other materials, this piece is often portrayed by a head or other part of the body in need of healing. Previously a symbol of faith, it is used today as an art piece associated with an old tradition.
